Climate Literacy Initiative

The Climate Literacy Initiative is a community-driven program dedicated to educating individuals about climate change, its impacts, and sustainable solutions. By providing accessible resources, interactive workshops, and local engagement opportunities, the initiative empowers people to make informed decisions that contribute to environmental sustainability. Through collaboration with schools, businesses, and policymakers, it fosters climate awareness, resilience, and grassroots action to drive meaningful change for a sustainable future.
